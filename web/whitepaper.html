Deterministic Category Projection in Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Amin Parva · Insight IT Solutions · prismrag@insightits.com

Abstract

Retrieval-Augmented Generation suffers from category bleed: vector similarity has no mechanism to enforce domain-specific taxonomy boundaries. Graph RAG partially mitigates this through corpus-derived graphs, but relationships remain statistical — not expert-defined. This paper introduces Tier-1 Deterministic Category Projection: a rule-driven, mathematically grounded remapping layer that embeds client-verified categories into vector space before indexing. We formalise weighted category inference, spherical blend geometry, Johnson–Lindenstrauss reduction, and contrast the approach with standard RAG and Graph RAG along determinism, auditability, and taxonomy ownership.

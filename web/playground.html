Step 2 of 4

Map your categories

PrismRAG uses a rules strategy to organise your content into categories before embedding. This improves retrieval precision — especially for domain-specific queries.

🗂
How mappings work: each row defines a keyword → category rule. When your content contains that keyword, those chunks get tagged with the category, which PrismRAG uses to route queries to the right neighbourhood of your index.

Skip this step if your content is general-purpose — PrismRAG will still work well with semantic-only retrieval.

Step 4 of 4

Chat with your data

Your index is ready. Use the chat panel on the right to ask questions in plain language — PrismRAG retrieves the most relevant chunks and the AI agent synthesises an answer.

🎯
How it works: every message you send triggers a PrismRAG search that returns the top-K most relevant chunks from your index. The AI then answers only using those chunks — it won't hallucinate facts that aren't in your data.

The sources chips below each reply show exactly which chunks were used. Click any chip to see the raw text.
